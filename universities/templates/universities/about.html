About

College Dreaming is a website built during our team's senior year (2020-2021) at the University of Cincinnati. Its main goal is to provide a more personalized and holistic approach to the college search. With that being said, anyone can use this website, though it is targeted towards high school students and their families or adults who are looking to obtain another degree, obtain their first degree, or just take a few classes alongside work. Please note: College Dreaming is for educational purposes only and is not a business entity. It is purely meant to meet the requirements to graduate from the University of Cincinnati.

Features

  • Google Maps integration - Allows you to virtually explore the surrounding area of a university/college. Letting you get an understanding of the type of environment surrounding a university/college.
  • Quiz Feature - Provides a more engaging and interactive college search. Instead of searching for a specific university or checking off on specific keywords, you will be presented with questions pertaining to potential interests and the result will be a university that accurately represents the interests.
  • Search Functionality - If you already know what universities/colleges you are interested in but canâ€™t seem to find all of the information in one place, you are able to search for a specific university/college and see all of the standard data pertaining to that university/college and non-standard data. A sample could be ACT/SAT scores, tuition, potential earnings after graduation, student demographics, nearby attractions, etc.
  • Advanced Search Functionality - This allows you to put weights on different variables related to a university/college such as crime, tuition, major, and much more. When you are done inputting your weights, a list of potential matches will appear that you can then click on and see more details.
