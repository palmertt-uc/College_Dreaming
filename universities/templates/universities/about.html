- About Us -

College Dreaming was created during the senior year of four students at the University of Cincinnati with the purpose of provide a more holistic college search experience while giving better representation to smaller, not as well known academic institutions. So go ahead, start your college search now.

- Features -

Over 6,000 Academic Institutions - We collected over data on over 6,000 academic institutions. This includes graduation percentages, major percentages, tuition, location, demographics, and more. No single institution has more representation than another, we guarantee you'll find an institution you've never heard of before.

Global University Search - Already know what academic institution you want to search for? Then click on the magnifying glass (when on laptop or desktop) and type in your institution's name.

University Quiz - Don't even know where you want to spend the next upwards to four years? Want something more than just a "tuition" search engine? Then take our University Quiz! We'll ask you questions and use your answers to find the one college or university that best fits your needs.

Favorite a University - We know the college search takes longer than just a day. That's why we added a way for you to favorite any colleges or universities that you really like. It's simple; create an account, click on the link sent to your email, search a college or university, click on their details and finally, click on the favorite button. That way when you come back to search for more colleges or universities, you can click on your profile and see what you've already favorited.
